üöó Driving Tips

  • Drive on the LEFT! - Arrows on road in high-risk areas
  • Speeds in km/h - 100km/h max on open roads
  • Use slow vehicle bays - let others pass on winding roads
  • One-lane bridges - give way if arrow points away from you
  • Fill up fuel when you see a station - gaps can be long
  • Watch for wildlife - especially dawn/dusk
